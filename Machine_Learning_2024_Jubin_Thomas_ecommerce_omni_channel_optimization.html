Ecommerce OMNI Channel optimization using AI

Video size:

Abstract

Delve into advanced AI and analytics strategies to optimize omnichannel order fulfillment, incorporating intelligent sourcing and inventory management. Witness tangible results knowing how machine learning can analyze your historical order data, and give a near to accurate prediction.
