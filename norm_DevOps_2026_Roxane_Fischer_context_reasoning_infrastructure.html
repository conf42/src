Context Engineering for DevOps: Turning Your Infrastructure into a Reasoning Graph

Video size:

Abstract

LLMs fail at infrastructure because they can’t capture live dependencies or multi-hop relationships. This talk shows how to treat infrastructure as a graph so AI systems can reason instead of guess. Learn Graph RAG, natural language to Cypher querying, and how to measure context quality.
