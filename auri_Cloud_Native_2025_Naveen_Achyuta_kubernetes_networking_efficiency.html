Optimizing Kubernetes Networking with Cilium and BGP

Video size:

Abstract

K8s networking, crucial yet often overlooked, can make or break cloud deployments. Learn how Cilium and BGP integration simplifies complex networking challenges. Through beginner-friendly explanations and practical use-cases, we will discover proven techniques to build reliable cloud-native networks
