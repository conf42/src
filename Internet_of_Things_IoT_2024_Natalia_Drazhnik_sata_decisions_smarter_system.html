From Data to Decisions: Bridging AI and IoT for Smarter System

Video size:

Abstract

AI is reshaping brand reputation management by turning social media data into actionable insights. My talk explores leveraging AI for sentiment analysis and trend detection to enhance customer engagement and build a reputation-focused, customer-first strategy.
