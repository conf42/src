Cloud-Native Data Lifecycle Governance for Trusted AI & BI

Video size:

Abstract

As AI-powered Business Intelligence (BI) accelerates cloud adoption, organizations face increasing pressure to implement end-to-end data governance across distributed, hybrid, and multi-cloud environments. In cloud-native architectures, governance can no longer be an afterthought—it must be embedded into the data lifecycle itself. This session presents a practical, research-backed blueprint for implementing cloud-native data lifecycle governance—from secure ingestion to auditable deletion—while maintaining performance and scalability. Drawing from real-world implementations, we explore how advanced lifecycle governance improves operational efficiency and reduces compliance risk. Attendees will learn how to integrate clear data ownership models, AI-powered classification, role-based access control (RBAC), and metadata-driven catalogs into cloud-native platforms. We’ll examine secure ingestion patterns and self-organizing frameworks that reduce latency, along with AI-enhanced validation techniques that strengthen anomaly detection. The session also covers tiered storage architectures and dynamic allocation strategies that balance cost, reliability, and compliance requirements. Finally, we explore automated retention and audit mechanisms that accelerate regulatory response times and enable consistent, policy-driven governance across cloud environments.
