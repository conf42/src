Subdomain hijacking - why DevOps is making us more vulnerable

Abstract

Subdomain takeover attacks are on the rise and they are becoming more prolific due to the decentralized approach to ops and embracement of SAAS services. In this talk we will introduce the problem, aimed at everyone from sysops gurus to absolute novices. We will then explain what an attacker can do and then finally how you can detect these conditions before the attacker and ideally before deployment! * What are subdomains takeover attacks and how is the adoption of DevOps ideologies increasing the risk of DNS misconfiguration? * What can an attacker do with a successfully hijacked domain? * How we can detect and defend DNS issues at scale
