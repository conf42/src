Introduction to SpinKube

Abstract

SpinKube streamlines developing, deploying and operating Spin WebAssembly applications in Kubernetes, resulting in delivering smaller, and more portable applications. The session will also introduce you to Spin and SpinKube, and compare and contrast the benefits and trade-off compared to containers.
