System-Level Experimentation at Scale: Pricing, Supply, A/B and Switchback Testing in FoodTech

Video size:

Abstract

Traditional A/B tests often fail in complex platforms when interventions like dynamic pricing affect the entire ecosystem, causing supply constraints and cross-user effects. Sergei Nasibian will introduce Switchback testing, a time-based alternative to user-level randomization that enables reliable system-level experimentation. Using real-world FoodTech examples, he will show how this framework optimizes metrics like Cost Per Order, courier efficiency, and order growth while aligning with operations and growth strategy.
