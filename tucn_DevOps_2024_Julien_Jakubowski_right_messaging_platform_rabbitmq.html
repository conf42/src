Choosing the Right Messaging Platform for Your Event-Driven Application: RabbitMQ, Kafka, or Pulsar?

Video size:

Abstract

Ready to elevate your game in event-driven apps? Learn how Pulsar’s unique architecture offers hassle-free scaling, multi-tenancy, and geo-replication while remaining compatible with RabbitMQ & Kafka. Unlock new levels of performance and seamlessly integrate Pulsar into your existing app portfolio

Summary

  • Stream native is a cloud native messaging and data streaming platform. Typical use cases for data streaming include data ingestion. Cloud native means that Pulsar is designed for running in containerized environments. It's not only about scalability, it's also about elasticity.
  • Pulsar is a unified messaging and streaming platform. It can handle both data streaming and messaging use cases. Do we have to manage two platforms, one for messaging and another for data streaming? With Prusan you can do both using the same technology.
  • Prusan is both scalable and elastic. The scalability requirements are determined by the bottleneck you have to address. Elasticity means you can grow or shrink resources quickly to adapt to workload changes. This can save on infrastructure costs by avoiding over provisioning.
  • In Prusan, there are two types of nodes, the broker nodes and the bookie nodes. The advantage of prusan is that scaling consumer doesn't require complex operations like adding partitions. To ease the use of pulsar and Kubernetes environment with remote, we developed kubernetes operators.
  • Pulsar topics are made of segments and these segments contain messaging. Pulsar can distribute the segments to separate bookies. This architecture allows older data segments to be offloaded seamlessly. Georeplication provides disaster recovery.
  • Multitenancy allows different departments or teams within an organization to share a browser cluster while keeping their data isolated. This is not a hack or another layer on top of Prusa. Pulsar is designed for that.
  • When it comes to choosing a technology, open source is key. Some of the benefits of choosing an open source technology are sustainability, avoiding, vendor locked in and community support. Feel free to try out pulsar by downloading it or by installing the operators.
