Journey into Quantum: How do I get started on the journey to realise business benefits from quantum computing?

Video size:

Abstract

Quantum computing promises to be transformational. This talk reviews opportunities and threats; assesses progress along the path to useful quantum computers, and introduces qTAP, a quantum technology programme that will start business users on their quantum journey.

Summary

  • Digital Catapult is the UK authority on advanced digital technology. Future Technology Access program will give education, training in quantum computing. Three use cases for near term quantum devices are quantum optimization, quantum simulation of physical systems and quantum machine learning.
  • A quantum egg timer can be in a superposition of two states at the same time. It's parallel processing over mini qubits in superposition. That can give quantum computers huger benefits over classical computers for certain algorithms.
  • We're in the near scale intermediate quantum era. To get to quantum computers with millions of qubits, then we need fundamental breakthroughs in research. In the future, the technological challenges will be resolved. We'll see large, universal, fault tolerant quantum computers.
  • Key areas that have been identified as applications of quantum computing in the more nearer term are optimization, quantum simulation of physical systems and quantum machine learning. Digital catapult are running a quantum technology access program to upskill and educate end users. It's important for organizations to be ready for when quantum computing is to be adopted into organizations.
