Building Slack Applications with Bolt.js

Video size:

Abstract

Slack is a messaging app for business that connects people to the information they need. In this talk we’ll see how to extend Slack functionality by developing a custom app. We’ll use Slack’s SDK for Node.js, Bolt.js (https://api.slack.com/tools/bolt) and Salesforce as backend. The app will let us create and manage work travel requests on Slack, avoiding context switching and maximizing productivity. Are you ready to fly??

Summary

  • Alba Rivas talks about how to create Slack apps with Boltjs. Make a real time feedback into the behavior of your distributed systems. observing changes exceptions errors in real time allows you to respond instantly to get things working again.
