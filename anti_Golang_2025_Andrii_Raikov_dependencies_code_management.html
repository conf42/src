The "Rabbit Hole" of Dependencies in Golang

Video size:

Abstract

Dependency management culture is something commonly missed in many companies. Poorly managed deps cause broken updates, circular dependencies, and the dreaded “helpers” folder that turns into a black hole. Small decisions in package design can lead to massive tech debt. Learn together to avoid.
