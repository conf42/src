RuntimeGuard-AI: Cryptographic Compliance for High-Risk AI Pipelines

Video size:

Abstract

RuntimeGuard-AI enables provable EU AI Act compliance for high-risk AI systems in production environments. It instruments the full inference pipelinefrom prompt intake to output validationgenerating tamper-evident, hash-chained records per request. Each record captures executed controls, decision paths, and overseer verification using digital signatures and Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKPs), allowing regulators to validate compliance without exposing proprietary data. The system supports low-latency operation across NLP, vision, and multi-agent systems, with real-time bias detection and human-in-the-loop escalation. Cryptographic attestations and chain-of-custody tracking ensure verifiable oversight across distributed agents, aligning compliance with modern DevOps observability and auditability practices.
