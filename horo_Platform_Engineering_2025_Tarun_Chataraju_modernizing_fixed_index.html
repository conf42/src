Cloud-Native Platform Engineering: Modernizing Fixed-Income Index Systems

Video size:

Abstract

We transformed a legacy fixed-income index system from monolithic downtime nightmares to cloud-native success. Learn our platform engineering strategies: Kubernetes orchestration, microservices migration, and Infrastructure-as-Code that cut costs while boosting performance.
