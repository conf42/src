From Experiment to Enterprise: Building Scalable, Observable MLOps Systems on Google Cloud

Video size:

Abstract

Shipping ML is harder than training it. Learn how to move models from notebooks to reliable production with a scalable, observable MLOps framework on Google Cloud covering real-time, batch, and streaming inference, drift detection, CI/CD, and retraining.
