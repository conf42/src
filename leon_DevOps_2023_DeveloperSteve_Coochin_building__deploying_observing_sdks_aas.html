Building, Deploying and Observing SDKs as a Service

Video size:

Abstract

Maintaining and developing SDKs for a multiple of lang can be time consuming. The biggest issue is creating SDKs quickly and streamlining app based consumption. In this talk we are going to look at some ways to automate your own SDK gen service and then testing the outputted SDK.

Summary

  • Steve Coochin is speaking at Conf 42 DevOps 2023 on deploying, building and observing sdks as a service. He says a good API really needs to provide a valid service. Having video and sample app content available is always great to add extra value.
  • Open API generator is one of the most amazing open source communities I've ever contributed to. Allows you to load in an open API spec and be able to generate sdks in a variety of languages. Please contribute back if you can, because they do fantastic work.
  • Distributed tracing is an industry standard method to monitor and observe different aspects to internal microservice infrastructure. Being able to make sense from that multitude of microservice deployments across multiple or all those different regions helps keeps things all nice and organized for errors.
  • Always remember to keep your end users in mind as much aas you can. Work out what works and continually look at ways to refine the end to end process. Remember to monitor and trace everything you possibly can. And remember to be excellent to each other.
