Real-Time Embedded AI for Deterministic, Adaptive, and Sustainable Robotics

Video size:

Abstract

Robots need fast, efficient, and reliable AI especially on edge hardware. This talk reveals a practical framework that unifies sensor fusion, lightweight inference, and deterministic actuation to achieve millisecond accuracy and long-duration autonomy.
