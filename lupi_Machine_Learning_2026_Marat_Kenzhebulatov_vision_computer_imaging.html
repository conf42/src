Building Secure Backend Services with AI Agents

Video size:

Abstract

As AI agents become part of everyday engineering, building secure backend systems is more critical than ever. Marat Kenzhebulatov will show how to integrate AI agents safely in security-critical workflows, from code review and threat modeling to automated checks, without exposing sensitive data. Attendees will leave with practical guidelines, lightweight patterns, and a checklist for leveraging AI agents while maintaining real-world security and compliance.
