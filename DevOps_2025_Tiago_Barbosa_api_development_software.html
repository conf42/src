API-First Development: Supercharging Your Software's Potential

Video size:

Abstract

In this session I’ll explain why companies should build their products with an API-first mindset to foster innovation, improve communication across teams and improve team productivity. Having this approach is key for companies to survive and prosper. I’ll do a mix of slides and demos.
