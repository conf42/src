Leveraging Blockchain for Secure and Immutable Firmware Updates

Abstract

In an era where firmware security and distribution is of utmost importance, this talk sheds light on the a potential of blockchain technology in safeguarding firmware or software updates, ensuring trust, integrity, and the highest level of protection.
