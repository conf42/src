Protecting PHI in Machine Learning Development Environments

Video size:

Abstract

Machine learning in healthcare is exploding, but PHI exposure in development environments is driving massive breaches and costs. This talk delivers real data, proven controls, and a practical roadmap to secure ML pipelines without slowing innovation.
