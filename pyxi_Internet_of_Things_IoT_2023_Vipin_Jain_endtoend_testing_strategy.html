End-to-end testing strategy for IoT systems

Video size:

Abstract

IoT solutions are evolving, hence a comprehensive thought process is required to test them. There exists known testing practices and activities, but density & combination of technologies are changing. As solution, we deconstruct the system in layers & build Small automation around them.

Summary

  • Vipin Jain is a senior delivery manager and head of the QA department at Metacube Software. IoT is his second love after his job as a delivery manager. How to develop a strategy to test IoT end to end. In his talk he will give you certain suggestions from his experiences.
  • Why do we even need to end to end test IoT setups when we can test each aspect of the application? Testing become very changing as it involves many applications working in tandem. The challenges in IoT testing are different from the challenges we have faced in testing noniot applications.
  • A test plan should include a combination of unit testing, integration testing, and endtoend testing. The more complex the solutions is, the more important unit tests become. Use a blended approach for testing IoT, deconstruct the system into layers.
  • IoT testing is challenging, but it is not difficult. You have to upgrade yourself from your normal testings to IoT testing. You also have to upskill yourself from testing softwares to testing hardwares. I'm open to any kind of questions.
