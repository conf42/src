Keep Your Enemies Close and Your Secrets Closer

Video size:

Abstract

In this talk, Audrey will share how she and her team have been working to develop tools and techniques for disseminating strong security practices to all the developers across Microsoft, how to bake security into the pipelines to halt vulnerabilities at the door, and the benefits of pre-commit hooks
