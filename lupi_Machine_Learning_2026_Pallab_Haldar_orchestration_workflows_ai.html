Transforming Inventory Forecasting with Production ML

Video size:

Abstract

Inventory forecasting is a high impact problem where small improvements can unlock significant operational gains. In this session, we’ll explore how production grade machine learning can be used to move beyond static forecasts and spreadsheets toward adaptive, data driven inventory decisions.

We’ll walk through how real world demand signals, feature engineering, and continuous model retraining come together in a production ML system. The talk will focus on practical lessons learned deploying and operating forecasting models at scale, including data quality challenges, model drift, and integration with existing supply chain workflows.

Attendees will leave with a concrete understanding of what it takes to turn inventory forecasting from an analytical exercise into a reliable production ML capability.
