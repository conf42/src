Prompt Engineering: Challenges, Considerations, and Best Practices

Video size:

Abstract

Prompt engineering is a crucial skill for effective AI interaction. This talk explores challenges like ambiguity and bias, key considerations, and best practices such as clear instructions and iterative refinement. Learn to craft prompts that elicit accurate, relevant AI responses.
