Securely Unifying Deployments in an Organization for Increased Governance

Video size:

Abstract

Security in your pipeline is the first line of defense against vulnerabilities, breaches and compliance issues, like Captain America’s impenetrable shield. This talk explains how security can easily be integrated into your pipeline so that your team can safely build, deploy and grow at every stage.
