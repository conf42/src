Abstract

Let’s kick off by examining how AI is reshaping the world of IoT, expanding it into the ‘Internet of Everything.’ Next, we’ll dive into the transformative impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI) on DevOps and explore how AI is revolutionizing development and operations processes, optimizing software development, and streamlining deployment methods, especially in AIoT. Then we’ll shift our attention to AI security, more focus on AIoT, where we’ll unravel the challenges and strategies needed to safeguard this interconnected landscape. We’ll examine how AI plays a pivotal role in ensuring the Information security and the security of IoT devices and data.
