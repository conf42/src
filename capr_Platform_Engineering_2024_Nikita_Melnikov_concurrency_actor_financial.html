Effortless Concurrency: Leveraging the Actor Model in Financial Transaction Systems

Video size:

Abstract

Discover how our startup leverages the Actor model with Golang and Kafka to revolutionize financial transaction processing. Dive into real-world applications and learn how to simplify and scale systems effortlessly, proving that the best concurrency is no concurrency.
