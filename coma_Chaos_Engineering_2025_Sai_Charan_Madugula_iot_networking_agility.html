Empowering IoT and 5G with Software-Defined Networking: A Blueprint for Agility, Scalability, and Intelligence

Abstract

Discover how Software-Defined Networking (SDN) revolutionizes IoT and 5G! From enabling smart cities to powering ultra-fast, low-latency 5G, this talk unveils SDN’s role in scalability and intelligence. Learn actionable strategies to build the backbone of a hyper-connected, intelligent future.
