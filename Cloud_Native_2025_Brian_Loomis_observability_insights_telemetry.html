Unlocking Business Insights with OpenTelemetry: Beyond Logging for Cloud-Native Applications like Chef360™

Video size:

Abstract

Logs and tracing are fine for debugging but fall short when trying to manage multiple customers operationally in software-as-a-service. We added OTEL to allow the business to price services on a consumption basis, show customers what they were using, and manage clusters for performance.
