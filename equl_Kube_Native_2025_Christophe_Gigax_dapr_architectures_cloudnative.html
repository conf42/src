Shaping next generations of architectures with Dapr

Video size:

Abstract

Building distributed microservices is tough. Dapr, an open‑source app abstraction layer, streamlines microservice development by exposing features like state management, pub/sub, and more via standard APIs. Tech‑agnostic and Kubernetes‑friendly, it simplifies orchestration and delivery.
