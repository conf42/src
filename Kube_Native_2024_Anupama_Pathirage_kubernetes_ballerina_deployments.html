From Code to Cloud: Mastering Kubernetes Deployments with Ballerina

Video size:

Abstract

Discover how Ballerina, an open-source language, simplifies cloud-native development by integrating deployment support directly into your code. With built-in Kubernetes capabilities, Ballerina automates the creation of Dockerfiles and Kubernetes YAMLs, streamlining your journey from code to cloud.
