AI-Driven Customer Experience: How to Start, Scale, and Succeed in the Enterprise

Video size:

Abstract

This session will highlight practical strategies for using AI to transform customer experience in the enterprise. Based on my work at Snowflake and supported by real-world outcomes, topics will include:

  • Building hyper-personalized customer journeys using AI in industries like retail, finance, and healthcare
  • Case study: Using AI to analyze CSAT feedback and dramatically improve response times and satisfaction
  • Leveraging modern AI agents and chatbots for smarter, empathetic support
  • Predictive analytics for proactive issue resolution across telecom, SaaS, and manufacturing
  • Navigating ethical challenges, including data silos, bias, and over-personalization
  • Five actionable best practices for effective AI adoption

The aim is to provide attendees with a practical blueprint for embedding AI into their workflows, balancing automation with the human touch, and driving measurable impact.
