From Alerts to Answers: Modern SRE with Observability, AI, and Agentic Ops

Video size:

Abstract

Traditional monitoring tells us something is broken—modern observability tells us why. This talk explores how SRE teams can evolve from reactive alert handling to proactive reliability engineering using distributed tracing, golden signals, and AI-powered agents. We’ll discuss real-world patterns for reducing MTTR, eliminating alert fatigue, and using agent-based systems to triage incidents automatically in cloud-native environments.
