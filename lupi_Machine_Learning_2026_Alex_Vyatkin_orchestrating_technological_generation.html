Next-Generation AI Agents: Orchestrating Technological Evolution

Video size:

Abstract

AI is evolving from isolated models to autonomous agents that interact with complex environments. Aleksandr Viatkin will explain how next-generation AI agents, inspired by principles from neuroscience and active inference, can be orchestrated to solve real-world tasks efficiently and adaptively. Attendees will learn how these approaches can reduce computational overhead, improve interpretability, and scale AI beyond traditional deep learning architectures.
