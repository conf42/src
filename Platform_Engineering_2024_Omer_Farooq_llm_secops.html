LLM SecOps

Abstract

LLMs like ChatGPT and Bard revolutionize language processing but face security risks. My talk covers SecOps best practices, automation for data hygiene, and DevSecOps collaboration to secure LLMs and ensure a trustworthy AI ecosystem.
