Kubernetes audit log best practices

Video size:

Abstract

Keeping an eye on all of the activity in your Kubernetes cluster is crucial to modern cloud computing. With just one cluster, and a handful of engineers this can be done ad-hoc and often the Kubernetes native logging is adequate. At scale however, with multiple clusters, regions, clouds, and tens or hundreds of engineers, Kubernetes audit logging can become a nightmare. Learn how to do it the easy, and secure way with open-source tools.This talk will run you through the best practices of making sure your cluster is secure, and how to keep a birdâ€™s eye view on everything going on in your cluster, making achieving compliance standards like SOC2 and FedRAMP a breeze. This talk will focus on various open source tooling such as Teleport, OpenRaven, and Elastic.
