The CISO is Dead, Now What ?

Abstract

Being prepared for the unexpected is the responsibility of every security and operations team, yet it is seldom practiced or simulated in a world of competing priorities. The goals are simple: how do we restore operational functionality? Getting there can be complex. How can we create simulations?
