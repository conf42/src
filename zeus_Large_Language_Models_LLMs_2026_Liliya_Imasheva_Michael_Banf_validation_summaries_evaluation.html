An Advanced Validation Pipeline for Summary Evaluation in High-Stakes Environments

Video size:

Abstract

In this lightning talk, we delve into the technical intricacies of our comprehensive validation pipeline designed for evaluating the quality of generated summaries in high-stakes environments. We will explore the setup of the pipeline, which integrates traditional metrics such as BERTScore, ROUGE, and BLEU, alongside an innovative “LLM as Judge” method in order to assess summaries across five critical custom dimensions: completeness, conciseness, harmlessness, correctness, and readability. We will discuss the meticulous validation process, including the use of synthetic summaries evaluated by experts and the incorporation of negative control summaries to test robustness. Attendees will gain insights into the technical details of our approach, including the criteria for each evaluation dimension and the scoring system. Furthermore, we will share techniques that led to significant performance improvements, such as reiterating on initial human evaluations and the strategic use of LLMs. We will demonstrate how we measure an agreement between human feedback and the pipeline’s assessments, underscoring its reliability and potential for application in high-stakes environments.
