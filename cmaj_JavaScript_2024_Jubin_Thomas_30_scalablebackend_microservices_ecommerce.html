Building Scalable E-Commerce Backend with Microservices (Node.js, Express, MongoDB, AMQP)

Video size:

Abstract

The core of microservices architecture is idea of decoupling functionality into discrete services that can operate independently. Each microservice is responsible for a specific business capability and can be developed and deployed separately.
