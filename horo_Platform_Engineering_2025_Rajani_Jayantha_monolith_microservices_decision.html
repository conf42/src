Platform Engineering Decision Framework: Microservices vs. Monolith for Scalable Cloud Infrastructure

Video size:

Abstract

Learn a proven framework for choosing between microservices and monolith architectures that platform engineers use to build scalable, reliable infrastructure. Real case studies + practical decision criteria = better platform decisions.
