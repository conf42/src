Spec Driven Modernization: Modernize your Repository using Spec2Cloud and the GHCP Modernization CLI

Video size:

Abstract

Every enterprise has it: a sprawling legacy codebase that nobody wants to touch. Hundreds of thousands of lines of code written in frameworks nobody remembers, with business logic buried in layers of technical debt. Traditional modernization is slow, expensive, and risky. What if LLMs could do the heavy lifting  and do it safely, at scale? In this session, I’ll share lessons from real-world agentic code modernization projects  including a large-scale transformation at a major Portuguese financial institution  where we used Spec2Cloud and the GitHub Copilot (GHCP) Modernization CLI to drive spec-driven modernization at scale. We’ll cover: Spec-driven development: generating machine-readable specifications from legacy code as the contract for safe transformation Spec2Cloud: how it spec-enables your legacy codebase for LLM-driven cloud migration The GHCP Modernization CLI: orchestrating AI agents across analysis, transformation, and validation workflows end-to-end Multi-agent pipelines with RAG over the codebase for grounded, context-aware migrations Evaluation strategies: LLM-as-judge and automated metrics to verify the LLM got it right What worked, what broke, and what we’d do differently This isn’t a vendor demo  it’s a practitioner’s honest account of taking LLMs into production on a mission-critical modernization programme. You’ll leave with a realistic picture of where agentic AI genuinely accelerates modernization work, and where you still need human engineers in the loop.
