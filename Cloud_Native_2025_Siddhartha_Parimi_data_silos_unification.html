Abstract

Struggling with data chaos? Learn how data lakehouse architectures unify data lakes & warehouses for better management. Explore Apache Iceberg, Delta Lake & Spark to cut costs, eliminate silos & enable real-time analytics. Future-proof your data—don’t miss this talk!
