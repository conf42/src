KubeVirt Alchemy: Transforming VMs into Kubernetes Workloads

Video size:

Abstract

According to the CNCF Annual Survey 2023, only 39% said they use containers “for most or all production applications.” This indicates that many organizations have only partially migrated their applications to containers or have not even started. KubeVirt is a technology that bridges this gap.
