Why Good Models Fail After Deployment

Video size:

Abstract

Great models don’t usually fail because they’re wrong - they fail because the world changes after they’re deployed. This talk explains why strong offline performance is often a poor predictor of real-world success, and how feedback loops, biased data, and misaligned metrics quietly break machine learning systems in production. Learn how to build systems that survive the real world!
