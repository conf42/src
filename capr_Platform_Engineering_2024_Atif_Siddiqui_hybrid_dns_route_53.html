Conquering Hybrid DNS with Route 53 Resolver

Video size:

Abstract

Nobody cares about DNS until it stops working. As platform engineers, we should accept this prevailing apathy. At the same time, building a robust Cloud (AWS) platform that addresses DNS management over a hybrid network remains a crucial tenet in sustaining this omnipresent apathy.
