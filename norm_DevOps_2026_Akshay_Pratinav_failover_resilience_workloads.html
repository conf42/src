The Multi‑Workload Challenge: Predictable Failover Across the Stack

Video size:

Abstract

Outages happen. At Intuit, a Kubernetes-native platform makes region failover boring: services declaratively onboard; we pre-scale from live traffic, promote standby DBs, and shift routes via Route53/Istio—slashing recovery time and standardizing resilience for hundreds of microservices.
